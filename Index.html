About FR1ZEEN

FR1ZEEN is a powerful tool designed to help Chromebook users unenroll from their devices. With FR1ZEEN, you can easily remove managed extensions and settings installed by administrators, giving you full control over your Chromebook experience.

Key Features

  • Removes managed extensions
  • Eliminates administrator settings
  • Easy-to-use interface
  • No technical expertise required

How to Use FR1ZEEN

  1. Download and install FR1ZEEN on your Chromebook
  2. Launch the FR1ZEEN application
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to unenroll your device
  4. Enjoy your newly unenrolled Chromebook!
