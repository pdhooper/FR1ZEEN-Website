Fr1zeen

What is it?

FR1ZEEN is an expliot that can unenroll a school chromebook by booting from a USB from custom shim to a GUI that gives you the option to then unenroll the Chromebook.

How Fr1zeen was found

The FR1ZEEN exploit was discovered by a group of ethical hackers during a routine security audit of school Chromebooks. They identified a vulnerability in the update engine that could be exploited to remove enterprise enrollment, leading to the creation of the exploit.
